Implement the FULL official regulation for the 2026 FantaFormula1 season. This update requires changes to the database schema, the scoring engine, and the draft logic.

ARTICLE 1 & 2: Structure and Draft Order
Two Championships: Maintain two independent standings: Drivers' Championship and Constructors' Championship.

Participants: Fixed at 10 players over 24 Grands Prix.

Draft Uniqueness: In a single GP, no two participants can pick the same driver or the same constructor.

Draft Order:

GP 1: Random draw.

GP 2 onwards: Reverse standings order (the last player in general standings picks first).

Deadline: The draft must close strictly at the start of FP1 (Free Practice 1).

ARTICLE 3: Pool Constraints & Jollies
Driver Pool (22 total):

Each participant must pick every driver on the grid at least once during the season.

Driver Jolly: 2 Jollies per season. A Jolly allows picking a driver already used before (Max 2 total picks per driver).

Constructor Pool (11 total):

Each participant must pick every constructor exactly 2 times during the season.

Constructor Jolly: 2 Jollies per season. A Jolly allows a 3rd pick on a constructor.

Obligated Picks: If a user has only specific drivers/teams left to fulfill the 'at least once/twice' rule, the system must enforce these as mandatory picks to prevent draft blocking.

ARTICLE 4: Scoring System (FIA Official Data)
Qualifying Points:

Pole Position (Sunday Race): 10 pts.

P2: 8 pts, P3: 7 pts, P4: 6 pts, P5: 5 pts.

P6 to P10: 4 pts.

P11 to P16 (Q2/Q1): 2 pts.

P17 to P22: 0 pts.

Sprint Shootout: 2 pts ONLY to the Poleman.

Race & Sprint Points:

Standard Race: 25, 18, 15, 12, 10, 8, 6, 4, 2, 1.

Sprint Race: 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, 1.

Constructors: Sum of points from both drivers of the chosen team.

2026 Specials (Bonus/Malus):

Fastest Lap: +2 pts (regardless of Top 10 finish).

Overtakes Made: +2 pts each.

Overtakes Conceded: -1 pt each.

These apply to both Driver and Constructor standings.

ARTICLE 5 & 6: Special Cases & Tie-Breakers
Post-Race: Update scores based on official FIA DSQ or penalties.

Substitutions: If a driver is replaced by an external reserve, the reserve inherits the original driver's history and picks.

Tie-Breaker: If total points are equal, the player with the highest single GP score wins. If still tied, compare the second-highest GP score, and so on.
